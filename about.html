Our Story

Our Story

From humble beginnings to a thriving community of digital creators

Founded in 2018 with a simple mission

Macford Skills began when our founder, Macford Chiwasa, noticed a gap between traditional education and the practical digital skills needed in today's economy. What started as a small YouTube channel teaching basic Photoshop techniques has grown into a comprehensive platform serving over 50,000 monthly learners.

We've helped hundreds transition from complete beginners to professional designers, developers, and content creators. Our secret? Focusing on real-world applications rather than just theory, and providing the templates, tools, and community needed to succeed.

Today, our team of 12 passionate educators continues this mission, constantly updating our curriculum to match industry trends and employer needs.
