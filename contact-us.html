Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform

How can I access the free resources?

All our free resources are available in the Resources section of our website. Simply browse the categories, find what you need, and click the download button. No registration is required for free resources.

What payment methods do you accept for premium courses?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for our premium courses. All transactions are securely processed through our payment gateway.

Can I get a refund for a premium course?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all premium courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

How do I become a contributor or instructor?

We're always looking for talented instructors! Please send us your portfolio, resume, and a brief proposal outlining the course you'd like to teach to instructors@macfordskills.com. Our team will review your application and get back to you.

Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our premium courses after you've finished all lessons and passed the final assessment. These certificates can be shared on LinkedIn or included in your resume.
